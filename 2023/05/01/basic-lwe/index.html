The security of scheme is based on the difficulties of the following problem:

Definition(Learning With Error, LWE): For positive integers m,n,qm, n, qm,n,q, and β≪q\beta \ll qβ≪q, the LWEn,m,q,β\textnormal{LWE}_{n,m,q,\beta}LWEn,m,q,β​ problem asks to distinguish between the following two distributions:

  • (A,As+e)(\textbf{A},\textbf{As}+\textbf{e})(A,As+e), where A←Zqn×m,s←[β]m,e←[β]n\textbf{A}\leftarrow \mathbb{Z}_q^{n\times m},\textbf{s}\leftarrow[\beta]^m,\textbf{e}\leftarrow [\beta]^nA←Zqn×m​,s←[β]m,e←[β]n.
  • (A,u)(\textbf{A},\textbf{u})(A,u), where A←Zqn×m,u←Zqn\textbf{A}\leftarrow \mathbb{Z}_q^{n\times m},\textbf{u}\leftarrow \mathbb{Z}_q^nA←Zqn×m​,u←Zqn​.

We assume the that the problem is hard, i.e. the two distribution is indistinguishable. The LWE problem has been proven to have a reduction to the difficult problem in lattice problem.

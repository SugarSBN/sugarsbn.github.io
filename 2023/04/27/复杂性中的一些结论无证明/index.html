# Dichotomy Theorems

Every Boolean constraint satisfiable problem is either in PPP or NPNPNP-complete.

2SAT 、 Horn-SAT 、 XOR-SAT ∈P\in P∈P，else in NPNPNP-complete.

Bulatov['02] extends the theorem to the ternary constraint satisfiable problem (the size of alphabet is 3), such as 3-Coloring.

ternary constraint satisfiable problems are either in PPP or NPNPNP-complete.
